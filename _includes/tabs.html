Where to start?

Activity Providers

#### Activity Providers
![Booking Systems]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/activity-1.png)
Get your activities online You may already use a system to list your activities online. If you don't have one, it's quick and easy to get [one]({{ site.baseurl }}{% link bookingsystems.md %}).
![Open Data]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/activity-2.png)
Tick the box to open your data Find out if your booking system is [OpenActive-enabled] If your booking system isn't listed then you may need to ask them to create an OpenActive feed, or you may need to build your own.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Your data is now open Your activities are now being published as a "dataset" via a page on your system's website, for anyone to access, use or share.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Your activities are available everywhere From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through to communities and services that engage local people. They may from time to time provide feedback or suggestions on how you can improve your data. We'll publicise your data to make sure that as many people use it as possible.
[Get Started]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link getting-started.md %}){: .button-primary}

Booking Systems

#### Booking Systems
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Create Simple API You build a simple open API to allow customers to publish thier activity listings information openly using the OpenActive data standards as a " *dataset* "
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Amend your terms and build an opt-out If you don't own the data, either include some text in your T&Cs or add a tick-box in your system to allow your customers to turn open data publishing on and off.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Make a dataset page so people can find your customers' data Create a "dataset" page on your website, making your data available for anyone to access, use or share. This page is automatically indexed by Google and included in the OpenActive dataset directory.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Your customers' activities are available everywhere From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through to communities and services that engage local people. Data users may from time to time provide feedback or suggestions on how you can improve the data you allow your customers to publish. We'll publicise your data to make sure that as many people use it as possible.
[Get Started]( {{ site.baseurl }}{% link getting-started.md %}){: .button-primary}

Use Data

#### Use Data
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Understand what open data is available and how you can help Browse our status dashboard and map to understand the kinds of data available, and read our advocacy guidance to find out how to create more open data.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Decide where you want to put the data From websites and apps that attract millions of users, through to communities and services that engage local people, there are lots of ways of using OpenActive data. If you already have a website that engages an audience or an app or a tool, it might make sense to include the data there. It might also make sense for you to build a whole new app, website or service, depending on whether you're a long-term business or a short-term campaign.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Decide how you want to consume the open data There are many white-label widgets available from organisations who make it easy for you to feature open data on any website. There are also aggregators that provide one API to access all the data, and of course you can collect and display the data directly yourself.
![what_open_data.png]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/placeholder.png)
Engage with activity providers and the community Provide feedback on the data via GitHub or reach out directly to the activity provider or booking system. Join the OpenActive community and help use your local or commercial influence to get more data
