Intro Copy

Developments in data, social media, VR and AI are having a huge impact on lots of aspects of people’s lives, removing pain points and creating better experiences for customers.

In the sport and physical activity sector, services designed to get people more active often don’t offer the seamless experiences we are used to in other industries such as travel or entertainment. Information about what’s available is mostly contained in silos and little collaboration happens between the different stakeholders in the ecosystem, such as gyms and sport clubs.
